Grid vs Flexbox

Flexbox is made for one dimensional layouts and Grid is made for two dimensional designs

  • flexbox: three buttons inside a header
  • grid: entire layout in tow dimensions w/ rows and columns

Flexbox = content basis

Grid= Layout basis
